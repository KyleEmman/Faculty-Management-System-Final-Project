Faculty Management System

The web-based faculty management system's main goal is to empower all of its numerous users to manage their personal information efficiently. Its five main users are administrators, academics, department heads, research coordinators, and extension service coordinators. Through a process of continuous improvement, it seeks to accomplish and maintain its operational and business objectives. The announcement page, which enables users to see and add announcements for their peers to view, is one of its primary features.

Faculty

The system allows faculty members to register. The subjects taught can be changed and added. They can also add research as well (divided into ongoing, presented, and published). They can also provide extended services, in addition. Finally, users can view the announcements that the departmental head and the coordinators for research and extension have placed.

Department Head

The department head can manage the faculty of a department. They can also compile a list of academic institutions that provide particular courses. Additionally, users can publish announcements and any other events and news relating to the faculty.

Research Coordinator

The research coordinators have the ability to produce the required printed papers. They can further filter faculty members using research. They can provide an overview of faculty research, issue requests for papers, and post information about other research-related news and activities.

Extension Service Coordinator

The extension coordinator has the ability to provide the required printable papers. Additionally, they have an extension filter for faculty members. They can also produce a summary of faculty extension. Finally, they can also publish notices of calls for papers and other events and news pertaining to extensions.

Admin

The Admin can manage users (faculty, research coordinators, extension service coordinators, and department heads). Admin could also manage all necessary contents of the system.
